SHOPEASY ONLINE STORE — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

About ShopEasy
ShopEasy is an online retail company founded in 2019, specializing in electronics,
home appliances, and fashion accessories. We ship to over 25 countries and serve
more than 2 million customers worldwide. Our mission is to make online shopping
fast, affordable, and trustworthy.

ORDERS

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Browse our catalog, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay
using credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, or PayPal. Once payment is confirmed,
you will receive an order confirmation email within 5 minutes.

Q: Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement, as long as the
order has not yet been processed for shipping. To cancel, go to "My Orders" in your
account dashboard and click "Cancel Order." After 1 hour, please contact support
directly, and we will try our best to accommodate your request before dispatch.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS.
You can also track your order anytime by logging into your account and visiting
the "My Orders" section.

SHIPPING

Q: What are the shipping charges?
A: Shipping is FREE for orders above ₹999 (or $15 USD equivalent). For orders
below this amount, a flat shipping fee of ₹49 ($1.50 USD) applies. Express
shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional ₹199 ($3 USD).

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.
Metro cities typically receive orders within 3-4 business days, while remote
areas may take 5-7 business days. International orders take 7-14 business days.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we currently ship to 25+ countries including the USA, UK, Canada,
Australia, UAE, and most of the European Union. International shipping rates
and delivery times vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

RETURNS AND REFUNDS

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be
unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by the original invoice.
Certain categories like innerwear, perishables, and personalized items are
non-returnable for hygiene and customization reasons.

Q: How do I initiate a return?
A: Go to "My Orders," select the item you wish to return, choose "Return Item,"
and select a reason. Our courier partner will pick up the item from your address
within 2-3 business days at no extra cost.

Q: When will I receive my refund?
A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect
the returned item. The amount is credited to your original payment method.
For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds are issued via bank transfer.

Q: Can I exchange a product instead of returning it?
A: Yes, exchanges are available for size or color variations on eligible
products within 30 days of delivery, subject to stock availability.

PAYMENTS

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, RuPay, UPI, net banking,
popular digital wallets, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for orders under ₹10,000
in supported regions.

Q: Is it safe to use my credit card on ShopEasy?
A: Yes, all transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption and processed
through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways. We never store your full card
details on our servers.

Q: Do you offer EMI (installment) options?
A: Yes, EMI options are available on orders above ₹3,000 with select banks and
credit cards. EMI tenure options range from 3 to 12 months, with interest rates
determined by your bank.

ACCOUNT AND MEMBERSHIP

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email
address, and follow the link sent to your inbox to set a new password. The
reset link is valid for 24 hours.

Q: What is ShopEasy Plus membership?
A: ShopEasy Plus is our premium membership program priced at ₹499/year. Members
get free express shipping on all orders, early access to sales, exclusive
discounts, and a dedicated priority support line.

WARRANTY AND SUPPORT

Q: Do products come with a warranty?
A: Most electronics and appliances come with a manufacturer warranty ranging
from 6 months to 2 years, as specified on the product page. Warranty claims
are handled directly through the manufacturer's authorized service centers,
and our support team can help you locate the nearest one.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach us via this chatbot, email at support@shopeasy.example.com,
or phone at +91-1800-123-4567 (toll-free, available 9 AM to 9 PM IST, all
days of the week). Average response time for email queries is under 4 hours.

Q: Do you have a physical store?
A: ShopEasy currently operates as an online-only retailer. We do not have
physical retail stores, but we have regional warehouses in Chennai, Bangalore,
Mumbai, and Delhi to ensure faster delivery.
