SHOPEASY ONLINE STORE — RETURN, REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY
Document Version: 2.3 | Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. OVERVIEW
This policy outlines the terms and conditions governing returns, refunds, and
order cancellations for purchases made on ShopEasy. By placing an order, the
customer agrees to the terms described in this document.

2. RETURN ELIGIBILITY

2.1 Return Window
Customers may request a return within 30 calendar days from the date of
delivery, as recorded by our courier partner's proof-of-delivery system.

2.2 Item Condition Requirements
To be eligible for a return, items must meet ALL of the following criteria:
  - Unused and unworn, with all original tags attached
  - In original manufacturer packaging, including accessories and manuals
  - Accompanied by the original invoice or order confirmation
  - Free from any damage not present at the time of delivery

2.3 Non-Returnable Categories
The following categories are NOT eligible for return under any circumstances,
due to hygiene, safety, or customization reasons:
  - Innerwear, swimwear, and socks
  - Perishable goods (food items, plants)
  - Personalized or custom-engraved products
  - Digital gift cards and downloadable software
  - Opened personal care and beauty products

3. RETURN PROCESS

Step 1: Log into your ShopEasy account and navigate to "My Orders."
Step 2: Select the order and item(s) you wish to return.
Step 3: Choose a return reason from the dropdown (e.g., "Wrong size,"
        "Defective item," "Changed my mind," "Item not as described").
Step 4: Select a refund method: original payment method or ShopEasy Wallet
        credit (Wallet credit refunds are processed faster, within 24-48 hours).
Step 5: Schedule a pickup. Our courier partner will collect the item from your
        registered address within 2-3 business days, free of charge.
Step 6: Once the item passes quality inspection at our warehouse (typically
        2-3 business days after pickup), your refund will be initiated.

4. REFUND TIMELINES

| Refund Method            | Processing Time After Inspection |
|---------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| ShopEasy Wallet Credit     | 24-48 hours                       |
| Credit/Debit Card          | 5-7 business days                 |
| UPI / Net Banking          | 3-5 business days                 |
| Cash on Delivery (COD)     | 7-10 business days (bank transfer)|

Note: Actual credit to your bank statement may take an additional 2-3 business
days depending on your bank's processing time, even after ShopEasy initiates
the refund.

5. DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please report it within
48 hours of delivery through "My Orders" > "Report an Issue," along with photos
of the product and packaging. Verified claims qualify for a full refund or free
replacement, including waived return shipping, and are prioritized for
expedited processing (typically resolved within 3-5 business days).

6. ORDER CANCELLATION POLICY

6.1 Before Dispatch
Orders can be cancelled free of charge any time before the order status
changes to "Shipped." Cancellation requests submitted after this point cannot
be guaranteed, although our team will attempt to intercept the shipment.

6.2 After Dispatch
If an order has already shipped, customers may refuse delivery or initiate a
standard return once the item is received, following the process in Section 3.

6.3 Cancellation Refunds
Cancellations made before dispatch are refunded in full within 3-5 business
days to the original payment method. No cancellation fee applies.

7. EXCHANGES
Exchanges (e.g., for a different size or color) are available for eligible
products within the 30-day return window, subject to stock availability at
the time of request. If the desired variant is unavailable, a refund will be
processed instead.

8. RETURN SHIPPING COSTS
Return shipping is FREE for the following reasons: defective product, wrong
item shipped, damaged in transit, or item significantly not as described.
For returns due to "changed my mind" or incorrect size selection, a nominal
return pickup fee of ₹49 may be deducted from the refund amount.

9. CONTACT FOR DISPUTES
If a return or refund is rejected and the customer disagrees with the
decision, they may escalate the matter by emailing
escalations@shopeasy.example.com with their order number. Escalated cases are
reviewed by a senior support specialist within 2 business days.
